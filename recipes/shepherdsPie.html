Shepherd's Pie

Description

Shepherd's Pie is a comforting and hearty dish that combines a flavorful meat filling with a creamy mashed potato topping. This classic recipe is perfect for a cozy family dinner.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground lamb (or beef for Cottage Pie)
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 carrots, diced
  • 1 cup frozen peas
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 cup beef broth
  • 2 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 4 cups mashed potatoes
  • 1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic, and sauté until fragrant.
  3. Add the ground lamb to the skillet and cook until browned. Drain any excess fat.
  4. Stir in the diced carrots and cook for a few minutes until they start to soften.
  5. Add the tomato paste, beef broth, and Worcestershire sauce. Stir well and let it simmer for 10 minutes. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Stir in the frozen peas and cook for another 2 minutes.
  7. Transfer the meat mixture to a baking dish and spread it out evenly.
  8. Spread the mashed potatoes over the meat mixture, smoothing it out with a spatula.
  9. Sprinkle the shredded cheddar cheese on top of the mashed potatoes.
  10. Bake in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes, or until the top is golden brown and the filling is bubbling.
  11. Let the Shepherd's Pie cool for a few minutes before serving. Garnish with fresh parsley.
